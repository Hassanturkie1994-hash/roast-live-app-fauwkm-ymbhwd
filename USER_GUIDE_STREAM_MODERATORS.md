### What You Can Do

When you join a stream where you're a moderator, you'll see special controls:

**Mute Users:**
- Temporarily silence disruptive users
- They can still watch, but can't chat

**Timeout Users:**
- Temporarily ban users from chat
- Choose duration: 1 min, 5 min, 10 min, 30 min, 1 hour

**Remove Messages:**
- Delete inappropriate messages
- Keep chat clean and safe

**Pin Messages:**
- Highlight important announcements
- Pin rules or important info

### What You CANNOT Do

âŒ You **CANNOT:**
- Access the creator's earnings
- Change stream settings
- Access analytics or dashboards
- Moderate other creators' streams
- Ban users from the entire platform

### Your Responsibilities

As a stream moderator, you should:

1. **Be Fair** - Treat all users equally
2. **Be Consistent** - Apply rules consistently
3. **Be Respectful** - Use moderation powers responsibly
4. **Be Communicative** - Explain why you took action
5. **Be Helpful** - Help maintain a positive community

### How to Remove Yourself

If you no longer want to be a stream moderator:

1. Contact the creator who assigned you
2. Ask them to remove you from stream moderators
3. They can remove you instantly from the "Manage Stream Moderators" screen

## ğŸ†š Stream Moderators vs Platform Moderators

### Stream Moderators (You!)
- Assigned by **creators**
- Can moderate **specific creator's streams only**
- Have **limited permissions** (mute, timeout, remove/pin messages)
- **NO** access to dashboards or platform features

### Platform Moderators (Staff)
- Assigned by **HEAD_ADMIN**
- Can moderate **ALL streams on the platform**
- Have **broader permissions** (stop streams, issue warnings)
- **YES** access to Moderator Dashboard

**You are a Stream Moderator, not a Platform Moderator.**

## ğŸ“± How to Use Stream Moderator Powers

### During a Live Stream

1. **Join the stream** where you're a moderator
2. **Look for moderator controls** in the chat interface
3. **Tap on a user's message** to see moderation options:
   - Mute User
   - Timeout User
   - Remove Message
   - Pin Message

### Muting a User

1. Tap on the user's message
2. Select "Mute User"
3. Choose duration or permanent
4. Confirm
5. User is muted from chat

### Timing Out a User

1. Tap on the user's message
2. Select "Timeout User"
3. Choose duration (1 min - 1 hour)
4. Add reason (optional)
5. Confirm
6. User is timed out

### Removing a Message

1. Tap on the message
2. Select "Remove Message"
3. Confirm
4. Message is deleted

### Pinning a Message

1. Tap on the message
2. Select "Pin Message"
3. Choose duration (5 min - 1 hour)
4. Confirm
5. Message is pinned at top of chat

## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Can I moderate any stream?
**A:** No, you can only moderate streams of creators who assigned you as a stream moderator.

### Q: Can I see who else is a stream moderator?
**A:** Yes, when you open the "Manage Stream Moderators" modal, you can see all moderators for that creator.

### Q: Can I access the creator's earnings?
**A:** No, stream moderators have NO access to earnings, analytics, or settings.

### Q: What if I abuse my moderator powers?
**A:** The creator can remove you instantly. Serious abuse may result in platform-wide consequences.

### Q: Can I moderate multiple creators' streams?
**A:** Yes! Different creators can assign you as a stream moderator. You'll have permissions in all their streams.

### Q: Do I get paid for being a stream moderator?
**A:** No, stream moderators are volunteers. However, creators may choose to reward you with gifts or VIP membership.

### Q: Can I stop a stream?
**A:** No, only the creator and platform staff can stop streams.

### Q: Can I ban users from the entire platform?
**A:** No, you can only ban users from the specific creator's streams. Platform-wide bans are done by ADMIN or HEAD_ADMIN.
