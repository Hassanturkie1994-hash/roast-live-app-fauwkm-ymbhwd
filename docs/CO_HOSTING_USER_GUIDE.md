
# Co-Hosting User Guide - Roast Live

## What is Co-Hosting?

Co-hosting allows you to invite viewers to join your live stream as guests. Guests appear on screen with you, and their audio/video is broadcast to all viewers.

## For Hosts

### How to Invite a Guest

1. **Start your live stream** as normal
2. **Tap the "Add Guest" button** (person with plus icon) in the bottom controls
3. **Select a viewer** from the list
4. **Tap "Invite"** to send the invitation
5. **Wait for the guest to accept** (they have 20 seconds)
6. **Guest appears on screen** once connected

### Managing Guests

#### View Guest Controls
- **Tap the "Guests" button** (two people icon) in the top bar
- See all active guests and their status

#### Mute a Guest
1. **Long-press on the guest** in the seat grid
2. **Tap "Mute Mic"**
3. Guest's mic is disabled (they can't unmute)

#### Disable Guest Camera
1. **Long-press on the guest** in the seat grid
2. **Tap "Disable Camera"**
3. Guest's camera is turned off

#### Remove a Guest
1. **Long-press on the guest** in the seat grid
2. **Tap "Remove Guest"**
3. Guest is disconnected from the stream

#### Make Guest a Moderator
1. **Long-press on the guest** in the seat grid
2. **Tap "Make Moderator"**
3. Guest can now moderate chat

### Guest Seat Layout

The layout automatically adjusts based on number of participants:

### Locking Seats

To prevent new guests from joining:

1. **Tap the "Guests" button**
2. **Toggle "Lock Seats"**
3. No new guests can join (existing guests stay)

## For Guests

### How to Join as a Guest

1. **Watch a live stream**
2. **Receive an invitation** (notification appears)
3. **Tap "Accept"** within 20 seconds
4. **Grant camera/mic permissions** if prompted
5. **You're live!** Your video appears on the stream

### Guest Controls

#### Mute Your Mic
- **Tap the mic button** in your controls
- Your mic is muted (host and viewers can't hear you)

#### Disable Your Camera
- **Tap the camera button** in your controls
- Your camera is turned off (host and viewers can't see you)

#### Leave the Stream
- **Tap the "Leave" button**
- You're disconnected and return to viewer mode

## Troubleshooting

### "Failed to connect as guest"

**Solutions:**
- Check your internet connection
- Ensure camera/mic permissions are granted
- Try accepting the invitation again
- Ask host to send a new invitation

### "Guest video is frozen"

**Solutions:**
- Check guest's internet connection
- Ask guest to disable/re-enable camera
- Host can remove and re-invite guest
- Reduce number of guests

### "Audio echo or feedback"

**Solutions:**
- All participants should use headphones
- Reduce speaker volume
- Mute when not speaking
- Host can mute problematic guests

### "Invitation expired"

**Solutions:**
- Invitations expire after 20 seconds
- Ask host to send a new invitation
- Accept faster next time

### "Seats are locked"

**Solutions:**
- Host has locked guest seats
- Ask host to unlock seats
- Wait for a seat to become available

## FAQ

**Q: Can I join as a guest on web?**
A: No, co-hosting is only available on mobile apps (iOS/Android).

**Q: How many guests can I have?**
A: Maximum 9 guests, but 2-3 is recommended for best quality.

**Q: Can guests send gifts?**
A: Yes, guests can send gifts to the host like any viewer.

**Q: What happens if my internet drops?**
A: You'll be automatically disconnected. Host can re-invite you.

**Q: Can I decline an invitation?**
A: Yes, tap "Decline" when you receive the invitation.

**Q: Can I leave and rejoin?**
A: Yes, but host needs to send a new invitation.

**Q: Do guests earn from the stream?**
A: No, only the host earns from gifts and subscriptions.

**Q: Can I be a guest on multiple streams?**
A: No, you can only be a guest on one stream at a time.
