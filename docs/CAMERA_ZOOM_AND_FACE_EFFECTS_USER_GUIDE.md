## Technical Details

### How It Works

**Zoom Calibration:**
- Maps your device's camera capabilities automatically
- Ensures consistent zoom behavior across all devices
- No more "zoomed in by default" issues

**Face Detection:**
- Uses TensorFlow.js and BlazeFace AI model
- Detects your face in real-time (~30 FPS)
- Tracks facial landmarks (eyes, nose, mouth, ears)
- Applies effects only to your face
- 100% on-device processing (privacy-first)

### Performance

**Zoom:**
- Instant response (< 50ms)
- Smooth transitions
- No lag or stuttering

**Face Effects:**
- Runs at ~30 FPS on modern devices
- GPU-accelerated for smooth performance

## Troubleshooting

### Zoom Issues

**Problem:** Zoom doesn't seem to work
- **Solution:** Make sure you're tapping the zoom buttons, not swiping
- **Solution:** Check that camera permissions are granted

**Problem:** Zoom levels feel wrong
- **Solution:** This is now fixed! Zoom is calibrated to your device
- **Solution:** Try all three levels (0.5x, 1x, 2x) to see the difference

### Face Effects Issues

**Problem:** Face effects don't appear
- **Solution:** Make sure your face is visible and well-lit
- **Solution:** Face the camera directly
- **Solution:** Wait a moment for face detection to initialize

**Problem:** Effects are laggy or slow
- **Solution:** Close other apps to free up resources
- **Solution:** Ensure good lighting (helps face detection)
- **Solution:** Try a simpler effect (Smooth Skin or Beauty)

**Problem:** Effects don't track my face
- **Solution:** Move slower to help tracking
- **Solution:** Stay within camera frame
- **Solution:** Ensure adequate lighting

## FAQ

**Q: Do face effects work with all zoom levels?**
A: Yes! Face effects work perfectly at 0.5x, 1x, and 2x zoom.

**Q: Can I use multiple face effects at once?**
A: Currently, only one face effect can be active at a time.

**Q: Do face effects use my data or send info to servers?**
A: No! All face detection happens on your device. 100% private.

**Q: Will face effects drain my battery?**
A: Face effects use moderate battery due to AI processing, but it's optimized for mobile use.

**Q: Can I adjust the intensity of face effects?**
A: Effect intensity is preset for each effect. Custom intensity coming in future update!

**Q: Do I need internet for face effects?**
A: No! Face effects work completely offline after the AI model is loaded once.

**Q: Why does 0.5x zoom look different now?**
A: We fixed the zoom calibration! 0.5x now shows a natural wide-angle view instead of being zoomed in.
