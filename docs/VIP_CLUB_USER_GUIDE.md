## For Creators

### How to Create a VIP Club

1. **Stream for 10 Hours First**
   - VIP Club unlocks after 10 total hours of streaming
   - Check your progress in Stream Dashboard
   - This requirement ensures quality creators

2. **Go to Stream Dashboard**
   - Tap Profile ‚Üí Stream Dashboard
   - Scroll to "VIP Club Overview"
   - Tap "Create VIP Club"

3. **Configure Your Club**
   - **Club Name**: Full name (max 32 characters)
     - Example: "Hassan's Elite Squad"
   - **Badge Name**: Short name for badge (max 20 characters)
     - Example: "Rambo"
   - **Badge Color**: Choose from 12 colors
   - **Description**: Optional description of benefits
   - **Price**: 30 SEK/month (you earn 70% = 21 SEK)

4. **Your Club is Live!**
   - Same club appears in Pre-Live Setup
   - Same club active in Live Streams
   - Same club in Chat and Inbox
   - ONE unified club everywhere

### Managing Your VIP Club

**View Members:**
- Stream Dashboard ‚Üí VIP Club Overview
- See all members with their levels

- Remove members if needed

**Edit Badge:**
- Tap "Edit Badge" in Dashboard

- Changes apply everywhere instantly

**Send Announcements:**

**VIP Chat:**
- Inbox ‚Üí VIP Club Chats

## Frequently Asked Questions

### For Creators

**Q: How do I unlock VIP Club?**
A: Stream for 10 total hours. Check your progress in Stream Dashboard.

**Q: How much does it cost to create?**
A: FREE! No cost to create or maintain your VIP Club.

**Q: Can I have multiple VIP Clubs?**
A: No, you can only have ONE VIP Club. This ensures consistency.

**Q: Can I change my badge later?**
A: Yes! Edit anytime in Stream Dashboard ‚Üí Edit Badge.

**Q: What happens if I remove a member?**
A: Their level and progress are reset. They lose VIP access.

### For Members

**Q: How much does VIP membership cost?**
A: 30 SEK/month. Cancel anytime.

**Q: Do levels reset?**
A: No! Levels are permanent unless you're removed from the club.

**Q: Can I be in multiple VIP Clubs?**
A: Yes! Join as many creators as you want.

**Q: What happens if I cancel?**

**Q: How do I level up?**
A: Send gifts to the creator during live streams. Every gift counts!
